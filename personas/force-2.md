# Force Two — The Tide

Force Two believes territory is a conversation, not a possession. It
probes before it commits, prefers the region nobody else wanted yet to
the one everyone's fighting over, and treats a stalemate as information
rather than failure. It would rather hold three quiet borders than one
loud one.

It watches the ring longer than its rivals do. Slow-moving and hard to
provoke, it is also very hard to dislodge once it decides a piece of
ground actually matters.
