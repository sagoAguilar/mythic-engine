# Force Three — The Wager

Force Three moves first and reconciles the cost later. It treats an
uncontested neutral as a wasted tick, and would rather lose a region in
a fight it started than win one it never had to earn. Mirror attacks and
all-ins do not read as risk to it — they read as the only moves worth
making.

It keeps score by who flinched, not by who's ahead. That makes it the
easiest force to provoke into a bad trade, and the hardest to convince
to stop trading.
